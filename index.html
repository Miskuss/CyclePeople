About The Cycle People

With over 25 years of combined experience, we know bikes.
We are a group of bicycle enthusiastes who banded together to refurbish bikes so others could enjoy the ride.
Whether you stop in to rent a bike or need your bike serviced in our fully equiped bike repair shop, come visit The Cycle People.

Voted Best Independant Tourism Shop on the Island

Rent or Repair, we do it all!

Bike Rentals

A bicycle is a great way to tour the northern island at your leisure. Our bikes are fully refurbished and ready for the road.

With a wide range of quality refurbished bicycles it's easy to make cycling part of your Northern Island experience.

Check out our selection and prices.

Bike Repairs

From flat tires to a full tuneup, taking care of that mid-tour repair or rebuilding that old cottage bike, we can provide you the service that you need fast and at a reasonable price. We have a selection of new and used parts and anything else can be ordered.
